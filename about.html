ABOUT US

Founded in 2024 by brothers Gianni and Francesco Mancini, No Permit Records emerged from a simple conversation about organizing small events. When discussing the challenges of performing pop-up sets without permits, Gianni replied, “let’s throw it, No Permit.” The name stuck, embodying a spirit of independence and defiance that defines our label’s culture.

At No Permit Records, we embrace a DIY approach born out of frustration with the stagnation and risk-aversion of major labels. Gianni and Francesco decided to take matters into their own hands, dedicating themselves to the development and support of new and emerging artists. Our mission is to provide a platform where artists can thrive without the constraints and bureaucracy of traditional music industry pathways.

We are proud of the deep relationships we’ve cultivated within the music community, standing by artists and helping them grow. This dedication has not only garnered attention from up-and-coming talents but has also resonated with major artists who remember the challenges of starting out.

NO PERMIT RECORDS IS MORE THAN A LABEL.
IT’S A MOVEMENT.
